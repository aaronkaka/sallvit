Our Story

sALLvitTM [Solve It] was co-founded by Rob Gallant and Aaron Kaka in 2013. It all started with an overall dissatisfaction of available online search options. Rather than complain, we decided to take action and we are venturing with foolish optimism to provide a free solution engine powered by people enabled through technology.

sALLvitTM is intent on being a free web-based collaboration forum to solve problems via global crowdsourcing. The crowd can pose real problems as well as provide rapid solutions from never-before-explored perspectives across multiple disciplines of expertise. It is our belief that the world is ripe with answers to any/all problems, all that is needed is collaboration.

Amplified Vision

  • sALLvitTM provides a free web-based solution engine framework and forum to solve problems through crowdswarmingTM in near-real-time or under significantly compressed timelines. Further, sALLvitTM provides an innovative hands-on solution service to satisfy your unique requirements and problems.

  • Tailor search engine results to provide desired answers1,2, leverage underutilized human creativity3 to provide solution content4, and commonly unify5 the world through uncommon individual actions. 1 - 4 execution steps or less 2 – Improve existing search engine overall optimization by 1-10%; Narrow the field of view for more focused search results 3 – Focus on Non-Professionals, Out-of-Lane industry, Arts for Science and Science for Arts 4 – Focus on action, not dialogue; Pictures are worth 1000 words, Video is worth 1000 pictures 5 – Games designed to stimulate desired actions and/or behaviors to achieve solutions
  • sALLvitTM Search (or more aptly named More or LessTM) can replace your existing search engine….or not…it’s up to you. sALLvitTM Search conducts a federated search using all of the best available content on the web. Further, sALLvitTM Search algorithms enable your search experience to be expedited by including criteria relevant to what you’re looking for and by excluding extraneous or non-applicable search criteria via user-defined sliders. If the content exists on the Internet, our goal is to provide you a solution in 4 clicks/taps or less. sALLvitTM is unique in that when you cannot find what you’re looking for (we refer to this as reaching the “Edge or End of the Internet”), sALLvitTM’s crowd will provide real solutions to your problems. On occasion, sALLvitTM partners with innovators to develop technologies and solutions directly. We refer to this as our Enterprise Development Division.
