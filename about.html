Our Story

sALLvit™ [Solve It] was co-founded by Rob Gallant and Aaron Kaka in 2013. It all started with an overall dissatisfaction of available online search options. Rather than complain, we decided to take action and we are venturing with foolish optimism to provide a free solution engine - powered by people and enabled through technology.

sALLvit™ is intent on being a free web-based collaboration forum to solve problems via global crowd-sourcing. The crowd can pose real problems as well as provide rapid solutions from never-before-explored perspectives across multiple disciplines of expertise. It is our belief that the world is ripe with answers to any problem... all that is needed is collaboration.

There is nothing new in this world, there is only news of what the world already knew.

Rob Gallant, Co-Founder and CEO

It is our goal to recruit the global population to make anything and everything better! Anyone wishing to contribute to this effort is welcome to join the team and forward our vision! We need open source software developers, game designers and gamers, web administrators, and social media experts. However, our experience has taught us that EVERYBODY has something to offer that can benefit the cause so any skill set is welcome on the team.
