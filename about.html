Our Story

sALLvit™ [Solve It] was co-founded by Rob Gallant and Aaron Kaka in 2013. It started with an overall dissatisfaction of available online search options. Rather than complain, we decided to take action and we are venturing with foolish optimism to provide a free solution engine - powered by people and enabled through technology.

There is nothing new in this world, there is only news of what the world already knew.

Rob Gallant, Co-Founder and CEO

Anyone wishing to contribute to this effort is welcome to join the team and forward our vision! We need open source software developers, game designers and gamers, web administrators, and social media experts.

On occasion, sALLvit™ partners with innovators to develop technologies and solutions directly. If you're interested in collaborating together to make your hardest problems transform into an elegant solution, feel free to contact us.
